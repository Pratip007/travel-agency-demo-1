Himalayan Paradise (Pelling)

6 Days & 5 Nights | Gangtok - Pelling - Ravangla

Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Transfer to Gangtok
Arrive at NJP Railway Station or Bagdogra Airport. Scenic drive to Gangtok. Check in to hotel. Evening free to roam MG Marg. Overnight in Gangtok.
Day 2: Gangtok to Pelling via Ravangla
Transfer to Pelling via Ravangla Buddha Park. Enjoy the mesmerizing views of Mt. Kanchenjunga. Check in to your hotel in Pelling.
Day 3: Pelling Sightseeing
Full day sightseeing: Pelling Skywalk, Pemayangtse Monastery, Rabdentse Ruins, Rimbi Waterfalls, and Khecheopalri Lake. Overnight in Pelling.
Day 4: Pelling to Darjeeling
Scenic drive to Darjeeling (4-5 hours). Experience the lush green tea gardens on the way. Evening free for leisure at the Mall Road. Overnight in Darjeeling.
Day 5: Darjeeling Sunrise & Sightseeing
Early morning Tiger Hill sunrise. Visit Batasia Loop, Ghoom Monastery, HMI, and Zoo. Enjoy a Toy Train joy ride (optional). Overnight in Darjeeling.
Day 6: Departure
Transfer to NJP/Bagdogra for your onward journey. Tour ends with happy memories.
