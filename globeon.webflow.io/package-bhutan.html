Bhutan - The Happiness Kingdom

6 Days & 5 Nights | Thimphu - Punakha - Paro

Detailed Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Paro & Transfer to Thimphu
Welcome to the Kingdom of Bhutan! Upon arrival at Paro International Airport, you will be greeted by our guide.
  • Scenic drive to Thimphu (approx. 1.5 hours), the capital city.
  • Check-in at your hotel and freshen up.
  • Evening visit to Tashichho Dzong to witness the flag lowering ceremony.
  • Overnight stay at a premium hotel in Thimphu.
Day 2: Thimphu Sightseeing
Full day dedicated to exploring the wonders of Thimphu.
  • Visit the massive Buddha Dordenma Statue for panoramic valley views.
  • Explore the National Memorial Chorten and Folk Heritage Museum.
  • Visit the Takin Preserve (National Animal of Bhutan).
  • Evening leisure time at the Clock Tower Square.
Day 3: Thimphu to Punakha via Dochula Pass
A breathtaking drive across the mountain passes.
  • Stop at Dochula Pass (3,100m) to see the 108 memorial chortens and Himalayan views.
  • Descend into the fertile Punakha valley.
  • Visit the majestic Punakha Dzong, located at the confluence of Pho Chhu and Mo Chhu rivers.
  • Walk across the longest suspension bridge in Bhutan.
  • Overnight stay in Punakha.
Day 4: Punakha to Paro
Drive back to Paro with scenic stops.
  • Visit Chimi Lhakhang (Temple of Fertility) en route.
  • Arrive in Paro and visit the National Museum (Ta Dzong).
  • Explore Rinpung Dzong.
  • Evening free for shopping in Paro town.
  • Overnight stay in Paro.
Day 5: Hike to Tiger's Nest (Paro Taktsang)
The highlight of the trip!
  • Morning hike to the iconic Tiger's Nest Monastery, perched on a cliff 900m above the valley floor.
  • Enjoy spectacular views and spiritual vibes.
  • Hot stone bath experience in the evening (optional) to relax your muscles.
  • Farewell dinner.
Day 6: Departure
After breakfast, transfer to Paro Airport for your flight back home, carrying memories of the happiest place on earth.
