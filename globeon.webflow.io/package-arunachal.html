Mystic Arunachal & Tawang

7 Days & 6 Nights | Guwahati - Dirang - Tawang - Bomdila

Detailed Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Guwahati to Bhalukpong
Pick up from Guwahati Airport/Railway Station.
  • Drive towards Bhalukpong (border of Assam and Arunachal).
  • En route visit the beautiful Nameri National Park (optional stop).
  • Check-in to your hotel/resort in Bhalukpong.
  • Overnight stay amidst nature.
Day 2: Bhalukpong to Dirang
A scenic drive through the mountains.
  • Visit Tipi Orchidarium to see rare orchid species.
  • Drive to Dirang, a picturesque valley.
  • Visit Dirang Dzong and the hot water springs.
  • Overnight stay in Dirang.
Day 3: Dirang to Tawang via Sela Pass
The journey to the land of snowy peaks.
  • Drive across the majestic Sela Pass (13,700 ft).
  • Stop at the frozen Sela Lake for photos.
  • Visit Jaswant Garh War Memorial to pay homage to the brave soldiers.
  • Visit Nuranang Falls (Jung Falls).
  • Arrive in Tawang and check into your hotel.
Day 4: Tawang Local Sightseeing
Explore the rich culture and history.
  • Visit the famous Tawang Monastery (Galden Namgey Lhatse), the second largest in the world.
  • Explore the Urgelling Monastery (birthplace of the 6th Dalai Lama).
  • Visit the Tawang War Memorial.
  • Evening free at the local market.
Day 5: Bumla Pass & Madhuri Lake (Optional)
Adventure to the Indo-China border.
  • Drive to Bumla Pass (Permit required, subject to approval).
  • Visit the stunning Sangetsar Lake (Madhuri Lake).
  • Return to Tawang by evening.
  • Note: This excursion requires a local vehicle and extra permit cost.
Day 6: Tawang to Bomdila
Start your return journey.
  • Drive back towards Bomdila.
  • Visit Bomdila Monastery and majestic view points.
  • Overnight stay in Bomdila.
Day 7: Bomdila to Guwahati Departure
Drive back to Guwahati (approx 8-9 hours). Drop at Airport/Railway Station for your onward journey.
