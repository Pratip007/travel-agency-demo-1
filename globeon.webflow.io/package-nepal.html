Magical Nepal Excursion

6 Days & 5 Nights | Kathmandu - Pokhara - Chitwan

Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu
Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport. Meet our representative and transfer to your hotel. Evening free to explore Thamel market. Overnight stay in Kathmandu.
Day 2: Kathmandu Sightseeing
Visit Pashupatinath Temple, Boudhanath Stupa, Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple), and Patan Durbar Square. Experience the rich culture and history of the valley. Overnight in Kathmandu.
Day 3: Drive to Pokhara
Scenic drive to Pokhara (approx 6-7 hours). En route, view the Trishuli River. Arrive in Pokhara, the city of lakes. Check in to hotel. Evening boat ride on Phewa Lake.
Day 4: Sarangkot Sunrise & Sightseeing
Early morning drive to Sarangkot for a mesmerizing sunrise over the Annapurna range. Return for breakfast. Visit Davis Falls, Gupteshwor Cave, and Seti River Gorge. Overnight in Pokhara.
Day 5: Drive to Nagarkot
Drive back towards Kathmandu and continue to Nagarkot. Famous for its sunset views over the Himalayas. Overnight stay in Nagarkot.
Day 6: Departure
Wake up to a stunning sunrise. After breakfast, transfer to Kathmandu airport for your onward journey.
