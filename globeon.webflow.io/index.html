Explore North Bengal, the Gateway to the Eastern Himalayas.

Nestled in the foothills of the mighty Himalayas, North Bengal is a land of rolling tea gardens, misty mountain peaks, rich cultural heritage, and incredible biodiversity. From the world-famous Darjeeling tea to the one-horned rhinos of Jaldapara, experience the magic of Northeast India's hidden gem.
