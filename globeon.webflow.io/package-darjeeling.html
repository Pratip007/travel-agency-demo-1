Darjeeling & Kalimpong

5 Days & 4 Nights | Tiger Hill - Monasteries - Heritage

Detailed Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Transfer to Darjeeling
Upon arrival at NJP Railway Station or Bagdogra Airport, you will be met by our representative.
  • Scenic drive to Darjeeling (approx. 3 hours), passing through Rohini and Kurseong.
  • Visit a tea garden on the way for a fresh cup of Darjeeling tea.
  • Check-in at the hotel.
  • Evening free to stroll around the famous Mall Road and Chowrasta.
Day 2: Tiger Hill Sunrise & Local Sightseeing
Start the day early for an unforgettable experience.
  • Drive to Tiger Hill at 4:00 AM to witness the spectacular sunrise over Mt. Kanchenjunga.
  • On the way back, visit Ghoom Monastery and the Batasia Loop.
  • After breakfast, visit the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (HMI) and the Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park (famous for Red Pandas).
  • Visit the Tenzing Rock and the Japanese Peace Pagoda.
Day 3: Transfer to Kalimpong & Sightseeing
Drive to the quaint hill station of Kalimpong (approx. 2.5 hours).
  • Check-in at your hotel in Kalimpong.
  • Visit Deolo Hill, the highest point in Kalimpong, for panoramic views.
  • Explore majestic Durpin Monastery and Mangal Dham.
  • Visit Dr. Graham’s Home and local flower nurseries (cacti & orchids).
Day 4: Excursion to Lava (Optional) or Leisure
Enjoy a relaxed day or opt for a short trip.
  • Optional drive to Lava to visit the Lava Monastery and enjoy the misty pine forests.
  • Alternatively, explore the local market of Kalimpong for handicrafts.
  • Enjoy a peaceful evening overlooking the Teesta River valley.
Day 5: Departure
After breakfast, drive back to NJP/Bagdogra via the scenic Sevoke road. Bid farewell to the mountains with wonderful memories.
