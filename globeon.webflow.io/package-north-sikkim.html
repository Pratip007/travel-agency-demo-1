North Sikkim Expedition

6 Days & 5 Nights | Lachen - Gurudongmar - Lachung - Yumthang

Detailed Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Transfer to Gangtok
Arrival at NJP/Bagdogra.
  • Scenic drive along the Teesta River to Gangtok (Capital of Sikkim).
  • Check-in at your hotel.
  • Evening free to explore the vibrant MG Marg, try local delicacies like Thukpa and Momos.
  • Overnight in Gangtok.
Day 2: Gangtok to Lachen
Start the adventure to North Sikkim.
  • Drive to Lachen (approx. 6-7 hours).
  • Stop at Singhik View Point for Kanchenjunga views and Seven Sisters Waterfall.
  • Arrive in Lachen (altitude 8,800 ft) by evening.
  • Dinner and overnight stay in Lachen.
Day 3: Gurudongmar Lake & Transfer to Lachung
A thrill at high altitude.
  • Early morning (4 AM) drive to Gurudongmar Lake (17,800 ft), one of the highest lakes in the world.
  • Return to Lachen for lunch.
  • Transfer to Lachung, a beautiful valley village.
  • Visit Bhim Nala Waterfall en route. Overnight in Lachung.
Day 4: Yumthang Valley & Return to Gangtok
Valley of Flowers.
  • Drive to Yumthang Valley (11,800 ft). During spring, it blooms with Rhododendrons.
  • Optional excursion to Zero Point (Yumesamdong) where the road ends.
  • Return to Lachung for lunch and then drive back to Gangtok.
  • Arrive late evening. Overnight in Gangtok.
Day 5: Gangtok Sightseeing
Relax and explore the capital.
  • Half-day sightseeing: Rumtek Monastery, Do-Drul Chorten, and Flower Show.
  • Visit Tashi View Point for sunrise (optional).
  • Free time for shopping souvenirs.
Day 6: Departure
Transfer to Bagdogra Airport/NJP Station for your onward journey with memories of the majestic Himalayas.
