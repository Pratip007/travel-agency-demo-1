Dooars Wildlife Adventure

5 Days & 4 Nights | Lataguri - Jaldapara - Murti

Detailed Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Transfer to Lataguri (Gorumara)
Arrival at NJP Railway Station or Bagdogra Airport.
  • Drive to Lataguri (approx. 2 hours), the gateway to Gorumara National Park.
  • Check-in to your resort amidst greenery.
  • Late afternoon Jungle Safari (Jeep) in Gorumara to spot Peacocks, Bisons, and Elephants.
  • Evening free to enjoy the resort ambiance or tribal dance (optional at resort).
Day 2: Excursion to Samsing, Suntalekhola & Rocky Island
A day amidst hills and rivers.
  • Drive to Samsing, famous for its tea gardens and orange orchards.
  • Short hike to the hanging bridge at Suntalekhola.
  • Visit Rocky Island, located on the banks of the Murti River.
  • Return to Lataguri. Evening free.
Day 3: Transfer to Jaldapara via Murti
Journey deeper into the wild.
  • Check out and visit the banks of the Murti River.
  • Drive to Jaldapara National Park (approx. 2 hours).
  • Check-in at the lodge in Madarihat.
  • Afternoon visit to South Khayerbari Leopard Rescue Center.
Day 4: Elephant Safari & Chilapata Forest
The ultimate wildlife experience.
  • Early morning Elephant Safari or Jeep Safari in Jaldapara to spot the famous One-horned Rhinoceros.
  • After breakfast, excursion to Chilapata Forest to see ruins of Nalrajar Garh.
  • Evening free for leisure.
Day 5: Departure
Morning free. Transfer to New Alipurduar Station (closest) or NJP/Bagdogra for your return journey, carrying memories of the wild.
